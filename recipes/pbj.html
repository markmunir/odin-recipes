Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A PG&J sandwich is a classic sandwich with bread, peanut butter and a jelly of choice.

Ingredients

    high quality bread
    peanut butter
    grape jelly

Steps

  1. Obtain 2 slices of bread
  2. On one slice, use a knife to spread peanut butter, covering one side of the slice.
  3. On the other slice, use a knife to spread the jelly, covering one side of the slice.
  4. Place the 2 slices of bread together, covered sides facing each other.
