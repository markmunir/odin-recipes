Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a pasta dish made from flat, broad pasta layered between meat and cheese layers.

Ingredients

    flat sheets of pasta
    mozzarella cheese
    red sauce

Steps

  1. Layer a sheet of pasta on a baking tray.
  2. On top of that sheet, layer the red sauce.
  3. Layer the mozarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  4. Repeat Steps 1-3 until the baking tray is filled.
