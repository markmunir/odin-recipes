Steak

Description

Steak is a cut of beef that is grilled and then basted with butter and herbs.

Ingredients

    steak
    garlic
    butter
    rosemary
    thyme

Steps

  1. Cook the steak on a hot grill for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Transfer steak to a cast iron skillet.
  3. Place butter and herbs into skillet.
  4. Baste the steak for another 30 seconds on each side.
